About VGC

Are you looking for a new challenge?
Perhaps you watched some battles from the Pokémon Championship Series and wondered how you could get involved, or maybe you're just looking to meet some new friends to play Pokémon with. It can be a lot of fun to participate in the Pokémon Video Game Championships, and participating in official Play! Pokémon tournaments is your chance to prove you really are the very best! The Pokémon Video Game Championships (VGC) lets you battle against Trainers from all over the world and compete for great prizes throughout the year.

The GSU Pokemon VGC Team needs strong players to represent us in online Pokemon tournaments.

  • Beginners are welcome

    All great players have to start somewhere. The GSU VGC Team is always looking to recruit beginners. Don't worry, we will show you the ropes.

  • No switch? No problem

    Although a Switch would be useful to have to play in official Pokemon tournaments, it is not required. We also participate in Pokemon Showdown Tourneys.

  • Format

    We will follow the curent VGC formats for official tournaments. Team members are also free to compete in Showdown tournaments and build teams with accordance to differnt Smogon tiers.

  • Team Meets

    Team will meet virtually for now. We will practice through YCOMM battles, hold team building workshops, discuss the current metagame, coordinate team events.

  • Team Spirit

    As VGC players we embody team spirit, collaboration, competition.

  • Unleash your competitive side

    We work hard and play hard. We compete to win!.
